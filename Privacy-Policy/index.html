Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how The Lamp RKP manages, protects, and uses the data collected from users of our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data from visitors. However, when users fill in their name during bookings or contact requests, we use it solely for recognition purposes and do not store it.

2. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies. However, we use Google Analytics to analyze user behavior and improve our website. Google Analytics may collect data such as location, device type, and browser type.

3. How We Use Information

The information collected through Google Analytics is used solely to improve the functionality and user experience of our website.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share user data with any third parties.

5. User Rights

If users wish to request data modification or deletion, they can contact us at contact@thelamp.site.

6. Security

We use HTTPS to secure our website and ensure data transmitted through our website is protected.

7. Compliance

We comply with Indian data protection laws and make reasonable efforts to ensure user privacy.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Please check this page periodically. Updates will be noted with a new "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@thelamp.site.
